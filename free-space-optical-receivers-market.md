<p><strong>The Free Space Optical Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Coherent</li><li>Coherent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Free Space Optical (FSO) receivers market is primarily divided into two segments: non-coherent and coherent systems. Non-coherent receivers operate by detecting intensity changes in the incoming light, making them simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for short-range communications. In contrast, coherent receivers utilize phase and amplitude information of the light wave, enabling higher data rates, longer distances, and improved performance in adverse weather conditions. Both types cater to different application needs in wireless optical communication.</p></p>
