<p><ul><li>Compound Essential Oil</li><li>Unilateral Essential Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The essential oils market is primarily divided into compound essential oils and unilateral essential oils. Compound essential oils are blends of multiple oils, designed to create synergy and enhance benefits for aromatherapy and therapeutic applications. In contrast, unilateral essential oils, also known as single-note oils, consist of a single plant extract, retaining specific characteristics and aroma. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with compound oils often used for complex scents and therapeutic effects, while unilateral oils are favored for their purity and simplicity.</p></p>
