<p><ul><li>Variable Bitrate(VBR)</li><li>Constant Bitrate(CBR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video encoders market primarily consists of two main types: Variable Bitrate (VBR) and Constant Bitrate (CBR). VBR dynamically adjusts the bitrate based on the complexity of the video content, leading to improved quality and efficient storage. This is beneficial for high-motion scenes. In contrast, CBR maintains a consistent bitrate throughout the video, ensuring predictable file sizes and stable streaming conditions, making it ideal for real-time applications. Both types cater to different user needs and content delivery methods.</p></p>
